This is brief description about my school

Adamjee Cantonment Public School (ACPS) is a well-known educational institution located in Dhaka Cantonment, Bangladesh. Established in 1960, the school provides quality education from primary to higher secondary levels. ACPS is affiliated with the Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education, Dhaka, and follows the national curriculum. It is known for its disciplined environment, focus on academic excellence, and emphasis on extracurricular activities, including sports, debating, and cultural events. The school has a large campus with modern facilities and is recognized for producing successful students in both academic and co-curricular fields.
